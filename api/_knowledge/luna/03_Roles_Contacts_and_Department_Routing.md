## Receiving / Packages

Receiving handles package and delivery-related matters.

Email:

**receiving@brickellhouse.net**

Use receiving for:

- Package pickup
- Delivery room questions
- Carrier questions

**admin@brickellhouse.net**

## Maintenance

Maintenance handles repair, technical, and common-area maintenance concerns.

Email:

**maintenance@brickellhouse.net**

Always copy:

**admin@brickellhouse.net**

Use maintenance for:

- AC / HVAC
- Plumbing
- Leaks
- Electrical
- Doors
- Locks
- Lighting
- Common area repair
- Amenities repair
- Gym equipment issue
- Trash room issue
- Odor
- Pest concern
- Housekeeping or cleaning concern
- Building equipment concerns

Approved routing:

“Please email maintenance@brickellhouse.net and copy admin@brickellhouse.net so the request can be properly reviewed and routed.”
