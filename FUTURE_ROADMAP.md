# Future Roadmap

The requested first five roadmap features are implemented at prototype or Sandbox-ready level.

## Production Blockers

1. Move products, orders, legal evidence, feedback, inventory, payment records, and statuses to Supabase.
2. Add Supabase Auth and role-based management permissions.
3. Test Square Sandbox through Vercel, including approved and declined cards.
4. Add Square webhooks so payment state is reconciled independently of the browser response.
5. Obtain and version attorney-approved Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy text.

## Later Features

- Management order notifications
- Resident confirmation emails
- Audit logs
- Resident/unit validation
- Refund and cancellation workflow
- Dashboard cards for daily orders, pending orders, monthly revenue, low inventory, and new feedback
